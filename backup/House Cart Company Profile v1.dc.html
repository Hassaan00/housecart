Chairman & Founder · House Cart

“We built House Cart to give Saudi businesses a food supply partner they can truly rely on.”

Saudi Arabia's food market is growing faster, and demanding more, than ever before. Retailers, hypermarkets, hotels, and restaurants need a distributor who understands quality, timing, and trust in equal measure.

We connect established international brands with the businesses that feed the Kingdom every day — with dependable supply, protected quality, fair pricing, and service that treats every partner as a long-term relationship.
